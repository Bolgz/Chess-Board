Description

This is my second small project using Javascript, HTML and CSS. There are a few bugs with the colouring of the board and the 'AI' opponent.

For the best experience, turn off the 'opponent' setting on the left and move some of your pieces. Keep the 'helper' setting enabled to see what valid moves your selected piece can make. Once you have selected a piece, click on a valid square to move it.

The 'select' setting will allow you to change the colour of selected pieces. The 'highlight' settings will allow you to change the colour of valid moves.
